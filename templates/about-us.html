Rebooks

Rebooks is a leading brand for second hand books, offered for sale in nine stores, in over 450 different points of sale (e.g., cafés, kindergartens, large companies, and gyms) and via Rebooks website and social media platforms. The vocational rehabilitation training process in Rebooks seek to improve hard and soft working skills including book collecting, sorting, sales (including on-line sales) and customer service.

Learn more

Mission

Our mission is to help people with disabilities, and specifically people with mental disabilities, to be included in society, to pursue their journeys, to reach their functional goals and to live independently in their community.

Vision

Our vision is to form a community where people with mental disabilities can pursue their dreams and goals, and live independently in their community .
